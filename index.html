GKR International School is a Co-Educational CBSE School, established as a Unit of Sri SelvaGanapathy Trust, with the vision of providing Quality and Value-Based Education. GKR International School has a truly Groundbreaking learning environment that presents exceptionally high standards of education to all the students. Every child deserves a vibrant, stimulating learning environment that encourages and supports their full potential. The GKR International School, spanning an area of five acres, offers a spectrum of facilities and resources that combines a world class education with distinctive, State-of-the-art Infrastructure. As students entered the school, they will be presented with the latest Technology-Based Learning resources that will fuel their senses.


The complete concept of the classroom is changing at Global International School. The smart classrooms at GKR International Schoolfeatures Wi-Fi connectivity, Interactive Whiteboards, Audio-Visual and Multimedia Projectors. It is this step forward taken by the GIS group which will declare the school as a “School of the Future”. At GKR International School, we believe that every step in education is essential. It has been scientifically proven that the pre-school years are the most important in students accomplishing their full potential and we look forward to partnering with parents along this journey.
